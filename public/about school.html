More About Our School

We hope that this will provide you with lots of useful information. If there is information that you are unable to find then please do not hesitate to contact us using the details shown in the ‘Contact us’ section. We will be more than happy to help.
Grace Action Faith International School (GAFIS) is located at Pankrono- Estate in Kumasi. an ultra modern I.C.T laboratory, comprehensive library, and spacious playground with the beautiful ornaments giving you a sense of appreciation of nature and fresh air. Moreover, there are three office complexes with CCTV cameras in all Offices and Classes. Our school currently starts from crèche to Junior High School three with an average student population of about 275. GAFIS now has a staff population of twenty three (23), comprising of seventeen (17) teaching staff and seven (7) non-teaching staff. GAFIS is all about the good facilities, deeper moral training which assist your ward to endure to victory. Good academic excellence with a conducive environment to support your ward. We admit children who are one year and above.

"In God we trust is our Motto": With transportation, the school has a school bus that conveys people from their various houses to the school and back to your home .Our buses are driven by highly qualified, experienced and God fearing drivers. Thus, ensuring the safety of your child. However, in academic competitions.We believe that all children should maximise their limitless potential and leave school having experienced a wealth of purposeful learning opportunities and feeling a strong sense of pride in the progress they have made. Our mission is for all children to flourish and excel and we are passionate about the development of the whole child. We recognise the importance of working together and pride ourselves on the positive partnerships that are created between children, parents and ourselves. Collaborative learning runs through all of our work and our curriculum encourages innovation, problem solving and creative thinking. The school enjoined Otumfoɔ Educational Fund Inter School Regional Spelling Bee competition with First runner up 2017. In co curricular activities, the school engages in other co-curricular activities that help to unleash the potentials of your ward. Some of these activities includes football, cadet, Discipleship Club, choreography and others. Important to note, these activities are well structured thus imparting the lives of these children.

Our ultimate goal is to teach students to live humanely within the community in order to prepare them to meet the demands and responsibilities of the future in the broader world.

Mission

VISSION

  • Creating an auspicious environment for the spiritual and natural life of each student.

  • Developing in the student an awareness of his/her responsibilities of the society

  • Providing what it takes to attain the highest academic excellence.

  • Putting in place a machinery in which each student can fully develop talents with which he/she may be endowed.
