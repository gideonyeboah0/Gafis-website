About Us

Grace Action Faith International School (GAFIS)

. Welcome to Grace Action Faith International School website. We hope that it will provide you with lots of useful information. We will be more than happy to help.
GAFIS is a privately owned institution located at Pankrono- Estate near True Vine Hospital in the Old Tafo Municipality, Kumasi in the Ashanti Region of Ghana. The school has a fantastic range of resources that all children access on a regular basis ranging from an ultra modern I.C.T lab, comprehensive library etc. We have a spacious playground with beautiful ornaments giving you a better sense of appreciation of nature and fresh air. Our size enables us to offer unique learning experiences and a well driven approach to ensure that all children are given the very best of learning opportunities.
