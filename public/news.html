GRACE ACTION FAITH RECIEVES AN AWARD

TThe Christian Transformation Officer(CTO) for the Ashanti Region Branch of EDIFY presented an award to our two contestants who partook in the just ended DISCIPLESIHP CLUB BIBLE QUIZ 2018 which Grace Action faith Internatonal School emerged second out of ten(10) Schools with (20) contestants. The 2018 Quiz marks the second edition of the DC quiz and the award was presented to the people by Apostle Micheal Adu Carol. In his speech as the guest speaker for the occasion adviced the children to take their morning devotion and entire bible studies serious as young people as he quotes from the Book of 1 Timothy 3:15 .However, he cautioned the pupils present to desist from all immoral acts but live a christ like character.

"Study to show your self approved unto God a workman that is not ashamed right dividing the word of truth"
1 Timothy 3:15.

However, he encoraged the pupil present to be law abiding wherever they find themselves, for that according to him was the true reflection of a true disciple of JESUS. In concluding his speech he encouraged the people to take their studies seriously so that they can excel greatly in their academic performance and with Christ holding their banner they shall succeed.
